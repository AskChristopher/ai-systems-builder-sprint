## Biggest Insight

Today I realized that my learning philosophy is becoming much larger than a custom GPT.

The focus is shifting away from AI itself and toward **augmentation**.

Technology should enhance human capability—not replace human thinking.

Learning should produce artifacts that demonstrate understanding.

The philosophy can be summarized in three ideas:

* **The Augmented Method**
* **Technology Enhances. People Create.**
* **Learning is complete when understanding becomes an artifact.**

I also realized that reading official documentation should become one of the core habits of my AI Systems Builder Sprint. Documentation is where understanding begins, and every documentation session should produce a working artifact, GitHub commit, or experiment.
