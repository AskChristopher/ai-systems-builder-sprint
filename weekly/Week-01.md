## Focus Guardrails

- Use a daily top-3 list before starting work.
- Work in 45-minute focus blocks with 10-minute breaks.
- If distracted, restart with a 10-minute minimum task instead of quitting the session.
- End each workday with a 3-line log: what shipped, what blocked me, what is next.
